## Auto Proposal Research Activity

Periodic review of streams to see what can be progressed to proposal stage, and creation of a proposal note with the relevant information and links to the source material in the stream.

This would allow for more effective discussions when council meetings are held, as the proposals would be more fully formed and have clear links to the source material and rationale behind them. It would also help to ensure that good ideas that come up in streams don't get lost or forgotten, and can be properly evaluated for their potential value to Archadia.
