# Pillars

## Overview

Pillars holds the internal knowledge of this island - the methodologies, frameworks, reference material, and domain knowledge owned and maintained here. It is the canonical source of truth for everything the island knows about itself and its subject areas.

---

## Aesthetics

[[Aesthetics/Aesthetics|Aesthetics]] holds the visual identity and design language of Knowledge Islands - the brand system, symbolic vocabulary, and asset library. It covers the visual style guide, the canonical symbol library, isometric tile sets for constructing territory maps, conceptual diagrams, and the logo.

## Philosophy

[[Philosophy/Knowledge Islands|Philosophy]] is the portable Knowledge Islands model - the concepts, conventions, processes, activities, agents, and tools that define how any Knowledge Island operates. Arcadia, as the canonical seat of the Knowledge Islands system, holds the authoritative definition here.
