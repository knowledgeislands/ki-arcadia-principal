## Three-Domain Model

The three core pillars are orthogonal concerns that must stay aligned - not hierarchical layers:

| Domain          | Concern                                                | Key question                  |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| [[Product]]     | Value - vision, strategy, priorities                   | Why and what are we building? |
| [[Development]] | Flow - delivery cadence, planning, work hierarchy      | When and in what order?       |
| [[Engineering]] | Quality - architecture, standards, technical practices | How well is it built?         |

Product sets direction. Engineering defines capability. Development manages execution.
