# Pillars

## Overview

Pillars holds the internal knowledge of this island - the methodologies, frameworks, reference material, and domain knowledge owned and
maintained here. It is the canonical source of truth for everything the island knows about itself and its subject areas.

---

## Aesthetics

[[Aesthetics/Aesthetics|Aesthetics]] holds the visual identity and design language of Knowledge Islands - the brand system, symbolic
vocabulary, and asset library. It covers the visual style guide, the canonical symbol library, isometric tile sets for constructing
territory maps, conceptual diagrams, and the logo.

---

## Knowledge Capital

[[Knowledge Capital/Knowledge Capital|Knowledge Capital]] holds the identity and configuration of this island - the charter, council
membership, activity roster, integrations, and island-specific operational values.

---

## Knowledge Islands

[[Knowledge Islands/Knowledge Islands|Knowledge Islands]] is the portable model itself - the concepts, conventions, processes, activities,
agents, and tools that define how any Knowledge Island operates. Arcadia, as the Knowledge Island of Knowledge Islands, holds the canonical
definition here.
