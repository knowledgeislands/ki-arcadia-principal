# Obsidian

## Overview

Obsidian is the primary human interface for the island - the editor through which notes are read, written, and navigated. It connects to the
island's text store directly via a vault, rendering wikilinks as a navigable graph and providing the editing environment for all note types.
The island's conventions in [[Notes]] and [[Library]] define what the vault contains; Obsidian is the tool through which those conventions
are enacted day to day.

---

## Templater

The Templater plugin scaffolds new notes to the correct format. Templates are a superset across all islands - not every template will be
relevant to every island. The full template index, with one entry per template and its purpose, is in [[Templates]].

Templates implement the structures defined in [[Format]] and [[Types]] - they are the Obsidian-layer expression of those conventions, not
the conventions themselves.
