# Charter

The Charter is the identity document at the heart of every Knowledge Island's `Admin/Governance/` zone — one of the two constitutionally required elements of island realisation (alongside the [[Council]]). It holds the fixed identity parameters that define an island (name, principal, task prefix, council membership), the adoption table recording each activity group's position (adopted or vetoed), and the configuration entries connecting adopted groups to their scheduled tasks and integrations.
