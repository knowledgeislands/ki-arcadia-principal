# Knowledge Capitals

The realisation layer of a Knowledge Island — the island-specific instantiation of the portable Knowledge Islands model. Two elements are
constitutionally required:

- **Charter** — the island's identity document: name, principal, prefix, council membership, and its adoption position on every activity
  group. See [Charter](Charter/Charter.md).
- **Council** — the governing body. Membership and terms are declared in the Charter.

Both live in the island's `Admin/Governance/` zone. The portable model definitions of these concepts live here in Pillars.
