# Tending

## Overview

Arcadia has adopted the Tending activity group. All nine activities are enabled - three scheduled automations (Scheduled Task Audit, Health Check, Knowledge Rebuild) and six conversational activities. See [[Charter]] for the adoption position and the full activity roster, and [[Schedule]] for the day-type taxonomy that the scheduled automations read.
