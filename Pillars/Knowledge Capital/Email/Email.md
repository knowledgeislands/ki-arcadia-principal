# Email

## Overview

Arcadia has vetoed the Email activity group. Email inbox management is not a function of this island — Arcadia is a knowledge repository and framework custodian, not an operational system with an inbox to manage. The veto is deliberate and is recorded in the [[Pillars/Knowledge Capital/Manifest|Manifest]].

The activity definitions for the Email group — Route Drift, Route Triage, Route Review, Re-route Triaged, Recap, and Email Test — are held in this island as canonical framework definitions under [[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Governance/Activities/Email/Email|Activities/Email]]. They exist here because Arcadia is the custodian of the Knowledge Islands framework, not because Arcadia has adopted them.

The notes below exist to fulfil the wikilink contract — framework notes link to these paths — and to make the veto explicit rather than absent.
