# Agents

## Overview

The operating layer: who and what acts on the island, and how. Two distinct modes are documented here — human operation (manual curation, navigation, and review) and agentic AI operation (automated modes, patterns, and memory). The tools both modes use are documented separately in [[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Governance/Tools/Tools|Tools]].

[[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Governance/Agents/Human/Human|Human]] covers manual operation of the island — GUI-based editing, curation, and review using Obsidian and other interfaces.

## Claude

[[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Governance/Agents/Claude/Claude|Claude]] covers how Claude operates as an agent within the island: the five operating modes, behavioural constraints, and memory architecture. ## ChatGPT

[[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Governance/Agents/ChatGPT/ChatGPT|ChatGPT]] is used as a read-heavy tool rather than an agent. Context is loaded manually; outputs are routed into the island by the user. No scheduled automations or direct write access. Documented here for structural consistency.
