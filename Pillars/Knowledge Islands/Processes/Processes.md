# Processes

## Overview

The Processes subtree holds the operational mechanisms through which governance is enacted in practice. These are not policies or conventions - they are the active procedures that move knowledge from idea to ratified content, and that govern how the Knowledge Islands model itself propagates across the ecosystem.

Two distinct processes are documented here. The **Enactment Process** is internal: it governs how any island moves a proposal through its own council to ratification. The **Contribution Process** is external: it governs how knowledge developed in one archipelago can be proposed as a canonical addition to the Knowledge Islands model in Arcadia. Both processes work in concert with [[Concept]], which defines the standing model - who counts as a Citizen, what council membership means, and what authority the council holds.
