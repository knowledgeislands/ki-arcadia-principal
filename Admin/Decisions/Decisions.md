# Decisions

## Overview

This folder holds the Knowledge Decision Records (KDRs) for Arcadia. A KDR captures a significant structural decision about this island --
its organisation, governance, tools, or cross-repo relationships -- in a permanent, standalone record. KDRs use the Nygard five-section
format (Context, Decision, Consequences) with a `type` field drawn from the six-value taxonomy defined in the `knowledgeislands-kdrs` skill.

Not every change warrants a KDR. Routine content additions, typo fixes, and minor configuration changes do not. KDRs are for decisions with
standalone standing: structural choices, adoption of formats or instruments, cross-repo boundaries. A KDR is typically the formal output of
an Enactment Process proposal whose `Decision` row is significant enough to warrant a permanent record.

Once `Accepted`, a KDR is immutable -- it is superseded by a new record, never edited.
