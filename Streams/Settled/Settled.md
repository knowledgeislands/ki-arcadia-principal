# Settled

## Overview

Settled streams are projects and workstreams that have reached a natural conclusion - completed, shipped, or otherwise finished. Unlike
[[Dormant]] streams (which are paused and may resume), Settled streams are closed. Notes are retained here as a record of what was done and
what was learned.
