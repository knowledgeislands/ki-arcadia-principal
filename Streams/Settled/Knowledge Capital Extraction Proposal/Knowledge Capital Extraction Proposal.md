# Knowledge Capital Extraction Proposal

**Settled: absorbed by GDR-KI-ARCADIA-003 and the capital terminology overhaul (2026-06-27)**

## What happened

This proposal was overtaken by two sessions that did the underlying work directly:

**Session: archipelago/territory separation (commit `16376d9`)** — separated archipelago (geography) from territory (governance) across the model. The word "Capital" was already present in these notes but never formally defined.

**Session: capital-as-governance-seat (commit `5ccfa16`)** — retired "Knowledge Capital" as a concept name for an island's realisation layer. "Capital" now means the governance-seat role held by a territory's principal island — not a folder or zone.

Key files updated in those sessions: SDR-KI-ARCADIA-003, SDR-KI-ARCADIA-005, Territories and Archipelagos, Glossary, How an Island Takes Shape, Jurisdiction, Charter, Authoring conventions, ki-website.

**GDR-KI-ARCADIA-003** is the formal decision record for the Admin zone restructure that this proposal anticipated: island-specific realisation (Charter, Council) now lives canonically in `Admin/Governance/`, not in a `Knowledge Capitals/` folder.

## What remains

~150 references in Streams proposals still use "Knowledge Capital" in the old sense. These are working documents; update each as it is enacted, not in bulk.

`Pillars/Philosophy/Realisation/Knowledge Capitals/` has been deleted. Its contents were all placeholders; no knowledge was lost.

## Authoritative definition

> The **Capital** is the seat of governance for a jurisdiction — not a folder or zone, but a role held by the principal island of a territory.
>
> Source: SDR-KI-ARCADIA-005, "The Capital" section; mirrored in `Territories and Archipelagos.md`.
