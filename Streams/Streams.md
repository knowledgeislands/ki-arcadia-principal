# Streams

## Overview

Streams are projects and workstreams organised by their current level of focus. Every piece of work in progress - or ever concluded - lives here. Streams move between sub-folders as priority changes; when a stream concludes, it is moved to Settled and may at a later date be removed and its notes subsumed into pillars.
