# Background

## Overview

Background streams are projects and ideas that are alive and being slowly pursued, but are not the current focus of attention. They surface periodically and may graduate to Active when circumstances change.

---

## Harbour, Routes and Customs

[[Streams/Background/Harbour Routes and Customs/Harbour Routes and Customs|Harbour, Routes and Customs]] tracks the work of completing the three unresolved elements of the KI geographic metaphor. All three are present in [[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Concept/Concept|Concept]] and [[Pillars/Knowledge Islands/Governance/Conventions/Structure/Structure|Structure]] but stub out at the point of specification - Harbour has conventions yet to be written, Routes and Customs exist as definitions without structural depth. The stream captures the open questions and provides a home for design thinking before the sections are written.
